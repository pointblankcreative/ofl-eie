Fed up with the rich getting richer, while workers struggle to get by?

We are too. We’re done with waiting and we’re taking matters into our own hands.

We’re taking action in a bold new way in our local communities. Because enough is enough. Here are our demands:

Real wage increases

As the cost of living increases, wages are staying the same. Here’s what we need to do.
